About HereNow

HereNow is a dynamic signage solution that brings discovery and delight to the travel experience by reconnecting people with place. Our mission is to empower people at all levels of experience to become experts on their surroundings and the systems that connect them to the rest of the city through improved access to real-time information.

Commuting can be a stressful experience involving unpredictable conditions, boring waits, and impersonal environments Often, the information available in our environment is unclear, generic, or available only to expert users who already know the system.
